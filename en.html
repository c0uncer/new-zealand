Our Products

OF MINE BLEND

Of Mine Blend is a mixture which you can feel the weight of espresso with the arabica beans from South America and Africa. We offer you an interesting taste which comes from the chocolate and caramel accompanied by some tropical fruits, citrus and currant.

GEITONAS

The word Geitonas, which means neighbor in Greek, is the place where coffee brings people together. We wanted to remember coffee, which brings people from all over the world around a cup of coffee, with chocolate and cocoa mixing with friut flavors. We recommend you try Geitonas, which is made of arabica beans only.

ALL BLACK

Almost from all of the American continent and the arabica beans from African countries are brought together in All Black, offering you a stronger experience. For those who want to experience the chocolate, hazelnut, honey, lemon and cocoa flavors come together in a stronger drink.

SIMPLE

While sipping the full-bodied single orgin Simple, roasted from African arabica beans, you can find yourself in an exotic environment. Simple waits for you with orange and sweet chocolate flavors in it.

FILTER COFFEE

Are you ready to witness unique tastes with NewZealand Coffee House? Now with renewed fall season glasses. You should try our filter coffee which we present with arabica beans from all over Africa. You can easily brew it at your home, office; and you can reach the maximum pleasure with the mixture of citrus, red fruits and raisins.

TURKISH COFFEE

We wanted to force the boundaries of the usual tastes with our Turkish Coffee, which consists of arabica beans only, making a more aromatic Turkish Coffee. We brought together the best beans in Africa and South America, mixing the chocolate and hazelnut-like flavors with citrus.
